# Multi-Camera Grid

<Warning>
  **Coming in Kaivue v1.x** — The multi-camera grid view is not yet available. This page describes how it will work when released. Check with your administrator or the [Kaivue changelog](../developer/api) for release timing.
</Warning>

The multi-camera grid lets you watch several cameras on one screen — useful for monitoring multiple areas at a glance without switching back and forth.

---

## How it will work

You'll be able to select 2 to 16 cameras and arrange them in a grid. The app automatically picks the best layout based on how many cameras you choose (2x1, 2x2, 3x3, and so on).

![Multi-camera grid with four live feeds arranged in a 2x2 layout](./images/placeholder-multi-camera-grid.png)

---

## Live streams vs. snapshot thumbnails

To keep your network and device running smoothly, the grid uses two different display modes depending on how many cameras you're watching:

| Number of cameras | Display mode |
|---|---|
| 1–4 cameras | **Live stream** — full motion video, same as single camera view |
| 5 or more cameras | **Snapshot thumbnails** — still images refreshed every few seconds |

When you have 5 or more cameras in the grid, each tile shows a regularly-updated snapshot rather than continuous video. This prevents overloading your network and device with too many simultaneous streams. Tap any tile to open that camera's full live view.

---

## What you'll be able to do

- Add cameras to your grid from your camera list
- Rearrange tiles by dragging them
- Save grid layouts as named views (for example, "Front Entrance" or "Warehouse Floor")
- Switch between saved layouts instantly
- Click or tap any tile to expand it to single-camera view
- Mute or unmute audio per tile

---

## When this ships

This feature is tracked as part of the Kaivue v1.x release cycle. When it becomes available, your app will update automatically (mobile) or you'll see a prompt to refresh (web). Your administrator will also be notified.

In the meantime, you can watch cameras one at a time using [Live View](./live-view).
