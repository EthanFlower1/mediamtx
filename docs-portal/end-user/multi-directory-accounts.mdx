# Multi-Directory Accounts

<Warning>
  **Coming in Kaivue v1.x** — The multi-directory account switcher is not yet available. Currently, logging into a new Recording Server replaces your existing session. This page describes how multi-account support will work when released.
</Warning>

If you have access to more than one Kaivue Recording Server — for example, your office building and a remote warehouse — you'll be able to add them all to the app and switch between them without logging out.

---

## Adding a second Recording Server

Once this feature ships, here's how to add another Directory to your account:

1. Open the app and tap your **profile icon** in the top-right corner.
2. Tap **Add Recording Server**.
3. Discover or enter the address of the second server (same options as when you [first set up the app](./getting-started) — auto-discovery, QR code, or manual address).
4. Log in with your credentials for that server.

The second server is now saved in your account list. You can add as many as your administrator allows.

![Account picker showing two Recording Servers with their names and status](./images/placeholder-account-switcher.png)

---

## Switching between accounts

Tap your **profile icon** at any time. A dropdown shows all your added servers. Tap any one to switch to it — the app loads that server's cameras without requiring you to log in again.

Each server's session is kept separate. Your camera lists, notifications, and settings are specific to each server.

---

## Signing out of one server

To remove a server from your list:

1. Open the account switcher (profile icon).
2. Swipe left on the server you want to remove (mobile), or click the three-dot menu next to it (web).
3. Tap **Sign out** or **Remove**.

This does not affect your session on your other servers.

---

## Signing out of all servers

To sign out completely:

1. Open the account switcher.
2. Tap **Sign out of all accounts** at the bottom of the list.

You'll be returned to the initial setup screen and will need to re-add your servers next time.

---

## When this ships

Multi-directory account switching is tracked for the Kaivue v1.x release. Until then, you can still connect to multiple servers — just log in to each one separately (logging into a new server will end your previous session).
